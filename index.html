The best thing about the herring sale is that 100% of the money goes to the kids.

We have the privilege to consult with doctors and oncology staff to find out which programs, activities and items not funded by the hospital are most needed. This helps us ensure that funds can go quickly and directly to improving the quality of life of kids with cancer.
