of the money must be spent on kids who are being treated for cancer at BC Children's Hospital.

The Steveston Herring Sale

The herring sale in Steveston will be held at the Steveston Harbour Authority.

TIME: 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM

Where: 12740 Trites Road, Richmond, B.C.

Be a Volunteer

We always have room for another volunteer and there is a wide variety of jobs. If you can run a wheelbarrow to carry a few bags of herring, take tickets from customers, or welcome customers and volunteers with a smile, we have a job for you. The herring sale is a happy place and you will be welcomed. For more information email fhkwcinfo@gmail.com

The Victoria Herring Sale

The herring sale in Victoria will be held at FAS Seafoods in downtown Victoria.

TIME: 7:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Where: 27 Erie Street, Victoria, BC
